Vision

To make STEM education accessible and inclusive for every child across Africa.

Mission

To inspire, train, and mentor young Africans in underserved communities through early STEM exposure, hands-on learning, and mentorship.

Program & Projects

STEM Clubs

We set up After-School STEM Programs, using a train-the-trainer model. Educators are trained to lead engaging, project-based STEM learning long after our pilot phase ends.

Moible STEM Hubs

STEM hubs that travel across communities to give children hands-on STEM experiences they might otherwise never have.

Learning Resources

We believe learning STEM should be exciting, creative, and engaging. That’s why we design edutainment resources such as comic books, animations and games.

Career Guidance

Connecting students with STEM professionals for mentorship, career talks, and scholarships.

After-School STEM Program Model

A sustainable model where we introduce a STEM Club, train teachers, hand over to the school, and support pupils to create annual projects solving real-life African challenges.

Our Leadership & Advisory Team

At STEMaChild Initiative, our team bring together diverse expertise from education, technology, development, and strategy, united by one mission: to make STEM learning accessible, engaging, and inclusive for every child across Africa.

Rahila Namah

Founder & Executive Director

Rahila is a Computer Engineer and technology enthusiast passionate about making STEM education accessible to every African child. A graduate of the Nigerian University of Technology and Management (NUTM), she combines technical expertise with a vision to build Africa’s next generation of innovators through early STEM exposure, fun learning, and mentorship.

Mr. Micheal Mu'azu

Advisor - Development & Policy

With over 25 years of experience across 10 countries, Mu'azu is a seasoned development expert and policy strategist. His work spans humanitarian aid, localization, and capacity building for NGOs. At STEMaChild, he supports strategy, governance, and sustainable development planning for impact at scale.

Mr. Olakunmi Agbaje

Advisor - Strategy & Partnerships

Kunmi is a strategist and architect with deep experience in maritime infrastructure, sustainability, and green innovation. He serves as Corporate Strategy Manager at Caverton Marine and COO of Rubera Consulting. At STEMaChild, he drives strategic planning and partnerships linking industry with education.

Mrs. Hanatu Baba

Advisor - STEM Education & Curriculum

Hannatu is an educator and digital literacy advocate with over a decade of experience. She currently leads foundation classes and digital literacy programs at Great Heights Academy, Abuja. She brings expertise in STEAM integration, teacher training, and storytelling-based learning to strengthen STEM education delivery.
