Privacy Policy &
Terms of Use

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this website.

Last updated: July 2026  ·  STEM Education for Children Foundation  ·  CAC 9418782

1. Who We Are

STEM Education for Children Foundation (“we”, “our”, or “the Foundation”) is a registered non-profit organisation in Nigeria (CAC 9418782) dedicated to bringing hands-on STEM education to children in underserved communities. Our website is stemachildinitiative.org.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact form submissions — When you fill in our contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent via Formspree (a third-party form service) to our email inbox. We do not store this data on our own servers.
  • Newsletter sign-up — If you provide your email address in the newsletter form, it is processed solely for the purpose of sending you updates about our work.
  • Cookies & local storage — We use your browser’s localStorage to remember your cookie consent preference. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and messages
  • Send you updates about our programmes and impact (newsletter only)
  • Improve our website based on how it is used

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

This website uses only a single browser localStorage key (stemed_cookie_consent) to store your consent choice. No third-party cookies are set. If you embed videos via YouTube in the future, YouTube may set its own cookies — we will update this policy accordingly.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Their privacy policy applies: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google’s servers, which may log your IP address. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps — an embedded map on our Contact page is served by Google. See Google’s Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as necessary to respond and maintain records. You may request deletion at any time by emailing info@stemachildinitiative.org.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. Contact us at info@stemachildinitiative.org.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
