Our Team

The people behind the mission

Diverse expertise in education, technology, development, and strategy — united by one belief: every child deserves access to STEM learning.

Founder & Executive Director
Rahila Namah

Computer Engineer and passionate STEM advocate who founded the organisation out of a conviction that STEM education, when taught through hands-on and relatable experiences, can unlock the potential of African children and empower them to solve real-world problems within their communities.

Advisor — Development & Policy
Mr. Micheal Mu'azu

25+ years across 10 countries in humanitarian aid, policy strategy, and NGO governance at scale.

Advisor — Strategy & Partnerships
Mr. Olakunmi Agbaje

Strategist and architect with expertise in sustainability and green innovation. Bridges industry and education to create lasting community impact.
